# WATonomous

## Role

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer on WATonomous, the University of Waterloo's
autonomous vehicle student team, since September 2025.

## Reinforcement learning and imitation learning

I train reinforcement-learning policies for locomotion and imitation-learning
policies for manipulation in Isaac Lab and PyTorch, logging rewards, trajectory
error, and failure cases to understand where policies break down.

## Computer vision

I fine-tuned a YOLOv11 computer vision model for competition-specific objects,
reaching 95% accuracy, and integrated it into our robotics simulation pipeline.

## Infrastructure

I use WATcloud's Slurm-managed GPU cluster for distributed training, which cut
our training time significantly.
